What is CoppaFeel!?

Bristol University Team

We're here to help everyone stand the best possible chance of surviving breast cancer by educating you on the importance of getting to know your boobs!

The Bristol Uni Boob Team represents the breast cancer awareness charity, CoppaFeel! We're a society with a difference, spreading a vital message while having loads of fun (and dressing up as boobs). Our role is to remind YOU to check your boobs regularly! We'll be spreading this message in fun ways here at Bristol, carrying out awareness and fundraising activities across campus.
