Welcome to Bristol

The University of Bristol CoppaFeel! Team

What happens at 23? Well, if you’re lucky, seeing a bit of the world, brunching with mates and a whole load of carefree fun. And if you’re less lucky? Breast cancer. Which is precisely what happened to Kris in 2009.

The Bristol Uni Boob Team represents the breast cancer awareness charity, CoppaFeel! We're a society with a difference, spreading a vital message while having loads of fun (and dressing up as boobs). Our role is to remind YOU to check your boobs regularly! We are on a mission to get every young person in the UK to check their boobs regularly in order to stamp out late and mis-diagnosis of breast cancer. We want to make coppin' a feel a habit of a lifetime. We'll be spreading this message in fun ways here at Bristol, carrying out awareness and fundraising activities across campus.
